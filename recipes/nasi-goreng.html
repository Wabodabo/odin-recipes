Nasi Goreng

Description

Nasi Goreng is a delicious Indonesian version of Fried Rice. Like any other fried rice recipe, it can contain a number of ingredients. This not only makes it a very easy dish to make, but also allows to you add anything you like specifically!

This recipe will be how my mother taught it, and it's delicious! In case you want to add anything else, feel free. The recipe below will be rather spicy, so if that's not your thing, just add less sambal.

Ingredients

Below ingredients will serve 2 people.

    Uncooked basmati rice (200g) 1 onion
  • garlic to taste
  • chicken thighs (300g)
  • 1 leek
  • Tauge
  • Sambal
  • Kecap Manis
  • MSG
  • Trassi

Steps

  1. Cook the rice using a rice cooker or according to the package. Preferably a day early
  2. Cut the vegetables and chicken into pieces
  3. Put a wok on high fire and wait until it is smoking. Then add the onion and Sambal, after first adding 2 tbsp of oil
  4. Once the mixture turns yellow, add garlic. Stir for 30 sec
  5. Add chicken and wok until golden brown.
  6. Add vegetables and wok till soft
  7. Add rice and remaining spices. Make sure to make the trassi very small.
  8. Finally poor Kecap manis on top till the rice has the desired color. Fry for a couple of minutes more
